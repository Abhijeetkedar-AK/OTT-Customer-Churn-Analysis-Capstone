## Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end Business Analyst solution for analyzing customer churn in an OTT (Over-the-Top) streaming platform. The objective was to identify the factors influencing customer churn, evaluate customer engagement and revenue trends, and provide actionable business recommendations through interactive dashboards and analytical reporting.

The project follows the complete Business Analysis lifecycle, beginning with requirement gathering and documentation, followed by data preparation, SQL-based analysis, dashboard development, and final business reporting.

---

## Business Problem

Customer churn is one of the major challenges faced by subscription-based streaming platforms. A high churn rate directly impacts customer retention, recurring revenue, and long-term business growth.

The business required a solution to:

- Understand why customers are leaving the platform.
- Identify customers likely to churn.
- Evaluate upgrade opportunities.
- Monitor business KPIs.
- Support management with data-driven decision making.

---

## Business Objectives

- Analyze customer churn patterns.
- Measure key business performance indicators.
- Identify customer segments requiring attention.
- Evaluate subscription performance and revenue.
- Analyze customer engagement and support interactions.
- Build an interactive Power BI dashboard.
- Provide strategic business recommendations.

# Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard tracks the following business metrics:

- Churn Rate
- Upgrade Acceptance Rate
- Total Revenue
- Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)
- Average Watch Hours
- Customer Rating
- Support Tickets
- Active Customers

---

# Business Insights

The analysis helped answer several important business questions:

- Which customers are most likely to churn?
- Which subscription plans generate the highest revenue?
- How does customer engagement influence churn?
- Which customers should be targeted for upgrades?
- How do support interactions affect customer retention?

---

# Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations were proposed:

- Improve engagement strategies for low-activity customers.
- Launch targeted upgrade campaigns for eligible customer segments.
- Monitor customer satisfaction through ratings and support metrics.
- Focus retention efforts on high-risk customers.
- Track KPIs regularly using the Power BI dashboard to support business decisions.
